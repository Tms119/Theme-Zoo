# Themes Zoo Customer Journey and Automated Flows

## 1. The Standard Purchase Journey (Crypto Checkout)
• The customer adds desired templates to their cart and proceeds to checkout.
• They provide their email address and select their preferred cryptocurrency for payment.
• If the customer applies a valid promotional code, the system calculates and applies the discount to the final total.
• A secure crypto invoice is generated via the payment gateway.
• The customer sends the required cryptocurrency to the provided wallet address.
• While the customer waits, the checkout screen securely polls the blockchain status.
• Once confirmed, the screen instantly transitions to a celebratory success view and unlocks the purchased files.
• The customer also instantly receives a beautiful "Order Confirmed" receipt via email containing a summary of their purchase and secure download links.

## 2. The Free Promotional Journey (100% Discount)
• The customer adds items to their cart and applies a special promotional code that covers the entire cost.
• The system recognizes the total is zero and completely bypasses the cryptocurrency gateway.
• The order is instantly verified and approved, displaying the celebratory success screen.
• The customer immediately receives a "Your Download Links" email containing secure access to their newly unlocked files.

## 3. The Abandoned Cart Recovery Automation
• A customer begins the checkout process by entering their email but closes the browser before sending the cryptocurrency.
• The order remains pending in the secure database.
• **Stage 1 (2 Hours Later):** A background worker automatically sends a helpful "Oops, did something go wrong?" email, offering customer support in case they had trouble with the crypto payment.
• **Stage 2 (24 Hours Later):** If the cart is still abandoned, the system sends a "Final Reminder" email. This email automatically includes a special discount coupon to incentivize them to complete their purchase.
• The system logs each stage, ensuring the customer is never spammed or emailed twice for the same abandoned cart.

## 4. Custom Service Request Flow
• A customer visits the services page, selects a custom package (such as a custom WordPress build), and fills out their project requirements.
• They complete the upfront payment using the same secure crypto checkout process.
• Upon blockchain confirmation, the customer receives a "Service Request Confirmed" email detailing their submitted project brief and confirming the next steps.
• Simultaneously, the administration team receives a blind carbon copy (BCC) of this exact email, instantly notifying them that a new high ticket client has paid and providing the full project requirements so work can begin immediately.

## 5. Automated Customer Support Routing
• A customer fills out a support ticket on the contact page regarding an issue or question.
• The system instantly sends an automated reply to the customer confirming receipt of their message.
• The administration team is secretly copied on this confirmation email, allowing them to read the customer request directly in their inbox and reply straight away.
